Carbonara

My personal specialty

ingredients

  • spaghetti
  • egg+egg white
  • guanciale
  • pepper
  • pecorino romano

steps

  1. boil spaghetti
  2. heat the guanciale in a pan
  3. beat the eggs
  4. once ready, mix spaghetti with guanciale
  5. add the grated cheese, pepper, and mix
  6. with the heat off, mix in the eggs
  7. enjoy
